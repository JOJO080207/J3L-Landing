Dual Military

From Combat Boots to Courts and Fields: Life, Leadership, and Raising Athletes the Military Way

About the Book

Twenty-five years in the Army. Eleven deployments. Two Sergeant Majors, two kids, and a family mission built on leadership, resilience, and grit. This book blends memoir with practical After Action Reviews (AARs) that parents, coaches, veterans, and leaders can use right away.

What You’ll Learn

  • Lead under pressure — at work and at home
  • Why consistency beats talent over time
  • How to mentor without micromanaging
  • Army lessons that work in civilian life

About the Authors

Joe & Lisa Birkhead are retired U.S. Army Sergeant Majors. They raised two driven kids through deployments, relocations, AAU sports, and family businesses — and now mentor others on leadership, resilience, and building winning mindsets.
